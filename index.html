Biography

I am currently a PhD student (2020-) at EEE, The University of Hong Kong, advised by Dr. Xiaojuan Qi. Before that I obtained my Bachelor's degree from Sun Yat-sen University, supervised by Prof. Liang Lin and Prof. Guanbin Li.

My research interests lie in machine learning and computer vision, with a particular focus on multi-modality and embodied AI.
